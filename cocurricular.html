Co-Curricular Activities at A. Haque School

science-club

The Science Club at A. Haque School ignites curiosity and fosters innovation among students. It provides hands-on experience in conducting experiments, solving real-world problems, and participating in science fairs. Through workshops, projects, and field trips, students develop a deeper understanding of scientific concepts and their applications in everyday life.

debate-club

Our Debate Club is designed to enhance students' public speaking and analytical skills. By engaging in structured debates, students learn to research topics, construct logical arguments, and present their viewpoints effectively. Regular competitions and practice sessions help students build confidence and improve their communication skills, preparing them for leadership roles in the future.

sports

Physical fitness is an essential part of a studentâ€™s overall growth. A. Haque School offers a variety of sports, including football, basketball, badminton, and athletics. Through regular practice and inter-school competitions, students develop discipline, teamwork, and resilience. Our well-equipped sports facilities ensure that students can train in a safe and encouraging environment.

music & arts

Creativity is nurtured through our Music and Arts Club, where students can explore their passion for music, painting, dance, and drama. This club provides a platform for artistic expression, allowing students to showcase their talents in school events and competitions. Professional trainers and workshops help students refine their skills and gain confidence in their artistic abilities.

community-service

A. Haque School instills a sense of social responsibility in students through the Community Service Club. This initiative encourages students to participate in charitable activities, environmental conservation programs, and awareness campaigns. By engaging in community service, students develop empathy, teamwork, and a sense of responsibility towards society.

coding-robotics

With technology shaping the future, our Coding and Robotics Club equips students with essential digital skills. Through programming, robotics challenges, and app development, students gain practical knowledge of coding and problem-solving. This club inspires young innovators to explore careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM).
