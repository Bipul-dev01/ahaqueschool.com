Message from Principal

Welcome to Amulia British International School, a unique and very special place unlike any other school. Situated at Demra, our school is nestled in a green valley with a large playground in the middle of our campus. We are committed to the academic achievement to all of our students.

We also believe in giving our students strong values along with a set of wings which may carry them far and wide. In other world, our students are equipped to face the challenges of the rapidly changing world. At ABIS you can feel the difference, and to appreciate just how special we are you must see us.

Zahid Hasan

Principal, ABIS
