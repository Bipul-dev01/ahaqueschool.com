Annual Events and Celebrations at
A. Haque School

Annual Sports Day

Annual Sports Day is one of the most awaited events at A. Haque School. It fosters a spirit of competition, teamwork, and fitness among students. The event includes various sports activities such as running races, football, basketball, and relay races, providing students with an opportunity to showcase their athletic skills.

National Day Celebration

National Day is celebrated with great enthusiasm at our school. Students participate in flag-hoisting ceremonies, cultural programs, and patriotic speeches. The event aims to instill a sense of pride and respect for the nation while educating students about its history and significance.

Cultural Festival

Our Cultural Festival is a vibrant celebration of diversity, creativity, and talent. Students perform traditional dances, sing songs, and showcase theatrical plays representing different cultures. This event fosters unity, appreciation of art, and self-expression among students.

Science Fair

The Annual Science Fair provides students with a platform to display their scientific knowledge and innovative projects. Students present experiments, models, and research findings, promoting curiosity and a love for science. Judges and visitors engage with students to discuss their ideas and encourage further exploration.

Teachers' Day Celebration

Teachers' Day is a special occasion dedicated to appreciating and honoring our educators. Students organize performances, present gifts, and express gratitude through speeches. This event strengthens the bond between students and teachers and acknowledges their invaluable contribution to education.

Graduation Day

Graduation Day is a significant milestone for students as they complete their academic journey. The event includes certificate distribution, farewell speeches, and performances to celebrate their achievements. It marks the beginning of a new chapter in their lives, inspiring them to pursue future goals with determination.
